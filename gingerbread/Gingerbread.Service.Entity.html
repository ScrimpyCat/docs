    Gingerbread vdev Gingerbread.Service.Entity

    Manages unique (optionally tagged) entities.

    Entities are unique IDs that are associated with an identity and can have a tag to help identify them. Entities can be connected to other entities in order to map out various relationships between the entities.

    An entities unique ID will be static and remain unique for the entire lifetime of the database. This means that destroyed entities, only destroys the entity itself but does not make that ID re-available.
